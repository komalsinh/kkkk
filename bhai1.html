BHAI

  1. We came into the world like brother and sister; And now let’s go hand in hand, not one before another.
  2. Because brothers don’t let each other wander in the dark alone.
  3. When brothers agree, no fortress is so strong as their common life.
  4. After a girl is grown, her little brothers, now her protectors; seem like big brothers.
  5. He who understands you is greater kin to you than your own brother. For even your own kindred neither understand you nor know your true worth.
  6. My brother is one of my true heroes. Steady and sober where I am impulsive and emotional.
  7. Nothing can stop me from loving my brother.
  8. There is no success you can celebrate more than the success of a brother.
